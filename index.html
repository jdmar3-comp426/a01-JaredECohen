The World of Magic The Gathering

The trading card game Magic The Gathering contains a massive amount of lore with each new set further build on this mythology. Magic The Gathering features a multiverse with many planes, each plane filled with characters and stories.

Learn about three pillars of Magic The Gathering's lore and power system: mana, planes, and planeswalkers.
