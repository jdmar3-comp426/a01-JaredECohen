Mana

Mana is the magic system in Magic The Gathering. Magic The Gathering has 6 types of mana: white, black, red, blue, green, and colorless. In the game, mana must be used as an energy source to play cards. In the Magic The Gathering universe, mana is the energy that powers all the magic.
