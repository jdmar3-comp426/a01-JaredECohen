Planeswalkers

Planeswalkers are among the most powerful beings in the Magic The Gathering multiverse. They are the only beings able to traverse between planes. Very few indiviudals are born with the planeswalker spark. In order to access their planeswalking abilities, this spark must be activated, and traumatic events are the most common catalyst for a planeswalker spark to ignite.

Jace Beleren

Jace is a human planeswalker and mind-mage from the plane of Vryn. Weilding power from blue mana, he is a master of telepathy and illusion.

Chandra Nalaar

Chandra is a human pyromancer from the plane Kaladesh. She is skilled in using red mana for her magic. Her planewalker spark ignited upon being moments away from execution, setting her off on a journey through the multiverse.

Kiora Atua

Kiora is a merfolk planeswalker from Zendikar. Relying on blue and green mana, she can control the sea and sea creatures. In order to fight the Eldrazi that threatened her home of Zendikar, Kiora traveled to Theros. In Theros, Kiora stole the goddess Thassa'a bident and assembled an army of sea creatures to fight the Eldrazi.
